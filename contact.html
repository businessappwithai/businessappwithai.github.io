Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about AppWithAI.

Do I need technical knowledge to use AppWithAI?

No. AppWithAI is designed for business analysts and domain experts. Simply describe your process in plain English, and the AI handles the technical implementation.

What happens if the AI doesn't understand my process correctly?

Our Human-in-the-Loop approach ensures you review and approve the data model before any code is generated. You can modify, add, or remove any entities, attributes, or relationships.

Do I own the generated code?

Yes. You own 100% of the generated code. There's no vendor lock-in. You can modify, extend, and maintain the code independently.

Can I integrate with existing systems?

Yes. Generated applications include standard RESTful or OData APIs that can integrate with any modern enterprise system, including SAP, Salesforce, Dynamics, and custom systems.

What about security and compliance?

AppWithAI generates enterprise-grade security including role-based access control, field-level security, row-level security, complete audit trails, and support for SOX, GDPR, and ISO 27001 compliance.

How long does it take to learn?

Most users are productive within hours. The interface is intuitive and designed for business users. Full training typically takes less than a day.

What if I need help after deployment?

We offer comprehensive support including email, priority support for Professional plans, and dedicated success managers for Enterprise customers. We also have extensive documentation and a community forum.
